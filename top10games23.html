Top 10 Video Games

Top 10 Video Games of All Time

  1. Super Mario Bros

    Description: A classic platformer game featuring Mario as he adventures through the Mushroom Kingdom to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser.

    Release Date: 1985

    Platform: Nintendo Entertainment System (NES)

    Publisher: Nintendo

    Genre: Platformer

    Developer: Nintendo

    My Personal Rating: 9/10

  2. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

    Description: A groundbreaking action-adventure game that follows the hero Link as he travels through time to stop the evil Ganondorf and save the land of Hyrule.

    Release Date: 1998

    Platform: Nintendo 64 (N64)

    Publisher: Nintendo

    Genre: Action-Adventure

    Developer: Nintendo

    My Personal Rating: 8/10

  3. Minecraft

    Description: A sandbox game that allows players to build and explore their own virtual world made out of blocks.

    Release Date: 2011

    Platform: PC, Xbox 360, Playstation 3, etc.

    Publisher: Mojang Studios (Microsoft)

    Genre: Sandbox

    Developer: Mojang Studios

    My Personal Rating: 9/10

  4. Tetris

    Description: A classic puzzle game where players must fit falling blocks into lines to clear them from the screen.

    Release Date: 1984

    Platform: Game Boy, Arcade, etc.

    Publisher: Nintendo

    Genre: Puzzle

    Developer: Aleksej Pajitnov

    My Personal Rating: 7.5/10

  5. Pac-Man

    Description: An arcade classic where players control the yellow character Pac-Man as he eats dots and avoids ghosts.

    Release Date: 1980

    Platform: Arcade

    Publisher: Namco Bandai Games

    Genre: Maze

    Developer: Namco

    My Personal Rating: 8/10

  6. Final Fantasy VII

    Description: A role-playing game that follows the story of cloud and the resistance group AVALANCHE as they take on the evil Shinra Corporation and the powerful Sephiroth.

    Release Date: 1997

    Platform: Playstation

    Publisher: Square Enix

    Genre: Role-Playing

    Developer: Square

    My Personal Rating: 9/10

  7. Grand Theft Auto V

    Description: An open-world game where players can freely explore and complete missions as three different characters in the city of Los Santos.

    Release Date: 2013

    Platform: Xbox 360, Playstation 3, etc.

    Publisher: Rockstar Games

    Genre: Action-Adventure, Open-World

    Developer: Rockstar North

    My Personal Rating: 9.5/10

  8. Half-Life 2

    Description: A first-person shooter game that follows Gordon Freeman as he battles the alien race called the Combine and discovers the truth behind their invasion of Earth.

    Release Date: 2004

    Platform: PC, Xbox, Playstation 2, etc.

    Publisher: Valve Corporation

    Genre: First-Person Shooter

    Developer: Valve Corporation

    My Personal Rating: 8/10

  9. Red Dead Redemption 2

    Description: An open-world action-adventure game set in the American Old West, where players control Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang, as they navigate through the decline of the Wild West era.

    Release Date: 2018

    Platform: Xbox One, Playstation 4, etc.

    Publisher: Rockstar Games

    Genre: Action-Adventure, Open-World

    Developer: Rockstar Studios

    My Personal Rating: 10/10

  10. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

    Description: An action role-playing game that follows Geralt of Rivia as he sets out on a quest to find his adopted daughter, Ciri, while dealing with political intrigue and facing powerful monsters.

    Release Date: 2015

    Platform: Xbox One, Playstation 4, etc.

    Publisher: CD Projekt

    Genre: Action Role-Playing

    Developer: CD Projekt RED

    My Personal Rating: 8.5/10
